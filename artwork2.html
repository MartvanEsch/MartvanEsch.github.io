TEDx, Lets Make

A fresh rebrand

For this rebranding project, I gave TEDx Let's Make a bold new visual identity inspired by the abstract forms and functional clarity of the Bauhaus style. Using imagery drawn from construction and architecture, the design reflects the core of what TEDx stands for: building ideas, shaping futures, and creating connections. The abstract aesthetic reinforces the message of innovation and progress, giving the conference a fresh, modern look rooted in timeless design principles.
