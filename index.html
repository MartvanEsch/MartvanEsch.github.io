About

As a kid, I always loved creating—whether it was with LEGO, in art class, or digitally. My first step into design was as a freelancer, making graphics for YouTubers, where I realized how much I enjoyed crafting visuals that fit a brand’s identity. Studying Communication & Multimedia Design deepened my passion for web and brand design, especially in creating interfaces that are both visually strong and functional.

In my free time I enjoy watching TV shows and movies, with Severance being a recent favorite. Football is another passion of mine—I support PSV and Arsenal.
