Spirit Day

Rebranding Paarse Vrijdag

This poster presents a new identity for Paarse Vrijdag: Spirit Day. The goal was to move away from the associations the old name can carry and give space to a broader, more inclusive message. Spirit Day puts the focus on presence, strength, and quiet solidarity — no labels, just support. The design strips it back: fewer symbols, more intention. A name that doesn’t ask for attention, but holds meaning for those who know why it matters.
