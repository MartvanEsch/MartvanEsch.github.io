Boxtel through my eyes

A living work of art

In this poster, I present Boxtel as I see it — not just a town, but a canvas full of artistic expression. From historical monuments and vibrant graffiti to striking modern architecture and peaceful churches, Boxtel tells its story through a blend of styles and eras. My design captures this unique fusion, reflecting how I experience my hometown: as an ever-evolving piece of art. This is my vision — Boxtel, seen through the eyes of a creative.
